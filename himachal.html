Himachal Pradesh

Here the Kullu and Kangra valleys rival all the natural beauty the veil of Kashmir can offer. Valleys and streams, snow-clad mountains and temperate forests offer a sportsman all he wants. Here, too, is India's present in the beautiful orchards that are the valley's wealth, and India's past in the ancient Rajput forts that are impregnable eyries atop the rugged crags that dot the area.

Highlights of The State...

Kullu

Known as the valley of the gods, Kullu is situated on the banks of the Beas River and is one of the most frequented tourist spots. Kullu is full of eye candies such as Great Himalayan National Park, Kaishdhar and Kasol.

Manali

Manali is situated near the end of the valley on the National Highway leading to Leh. The landscape here is breath- taking. From temples to antiquity, to picturesque sight-seeing spots and adventure activities, Manali is a popular resort for all seasons and for all travelers.

Shimla

With all its intricacies, history seems to have been the mortar for every brick and stone that has built Shimla. Places to visit include the famous Mall Road, the Ridge, Jakhoo Temple and the Glen.

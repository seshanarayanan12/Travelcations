Punjab

At the confluence of no less than five rivers, one will find the prosperous Indian state of Punjab. Blessed with these natural irrigators and some of the most fertile earth in India, Punjab holds court as one of India’s powerhouse agricultural states. Likewise, any tourist map of Punjab would tip off a realm with as much history as its harvests.

Highlights of The State...

Amritsar

Home to famous landmarks like the Golden Temple and the Jallianwala Bagh, Amritsar is one of the most ancient cities of the country. Also known as the gateway of India, Wagah Border which is only 29 kms is also a must see.

Chandighar

Chandigarh is well known city in India. It is also known as ‘City beautiful’. It was the first planned city with urban and modern architecture. Considered the most well-planned city in India, Chandigarh is also home to the famous Rock Gardens and the Leisure Valley

Jalandhar

Jalandhar is a lovely town amidst the Punjab region. Known for its top notch sport equipments and ethnic temples, Jalandhar holds a supreme place when it comes to sightseeing and visiting the Punjab region.
